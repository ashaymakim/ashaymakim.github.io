About Us

Established in the year 1984, Makim Med-Aids is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company engaged in manufacturing, exporting and supplying Plaster of Paris bandages. was the first company to manufacture Plaster of Paris Bandages B.P (P.O.P Bandages) in Gujarat, India. We have a team of skilled professionals who have conceptualized and designed the entire technology involved in manufacturing process. This enables us to deliver excellent products consistently along with significant economic versatility. Currently, we supply our bandages under the brand name of MEDICAST to several individual Doctors, Hospitals, several Government agencies (through tenders) as well as merchant exporters. Our firm has few competitive advantages such as experienced R&D department, large product line, large production capacity and customized product sizes. We are leading manufacturers and exporters of orthopaedic POP Bandages.

Our products

MEDICAST P.O.P conforms to the world-class British Pharmacopoeia Standards. Made from 100% cotton Leano Gauze Fabric of the finest quality,Medicast can withstand high tensions while being pulled in applying around limbs to be immobilized. The shrinkage of the bandages is also minimal.
All Medicast Bandages are wound in such a way so as to nest the core/ plastic mandrel with an extra turn. This ensures that the core stays in place throughout the application of the bandage. We are the only manufacturer to offer such an arrangement!!
